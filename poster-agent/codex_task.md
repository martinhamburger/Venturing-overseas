# Codex Task

You are helping produce a research poster in Figma for the topic `贸易战2.0背景下中国企业出海的应对策略研究`.

## Goal

Fill an existing poster structure with concise, presentation-ready content and produce a clean handoff for Figma implementation.

## Required Outputs

1. `poster_content.md`
2. `poster_layout_plan.md`
3. `poster_handoff.md`

## Constraints

- Do not redesign the overall layout from scratch unless the template changes.
- Preserve section hierarchy and reading order.
- Prioritize one clear takeaway over background detail.
- Keep numbers and named regions accurate to the local source material.
- Reduce paragraph density; prefer bullets and short blocks.
- Treat Figma as the final polish surface, not the first drafting surface.

## Working Order

1. Read `rules/`
2. Read `templates/`
3. Update `outputs/`
4. If visual structure changes, sync `poster/index.html` and `poster/styles.css`

## Default Narrative

The poster should argue that Chinese firms can no longer rely on simple export-led expansion under Trade War 2.0. Future competitiveness depends on choosing the right overseas mode by industry and building localization, resilient supply chains, technology depth, brand power, and global operating capability.
